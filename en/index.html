Who am I

Hello! My name is Łukasz! I currently live in Cracow, I deal with computer graphics, website design and programming - this is my main area interests. In addition, I'm interested in psychology and pedagogy - especially in the field self-improvement, emotions and interpersonal relations. I like being physically active outside of work, that's why I run, swim, walk to the gym, wandering in the mountains - depending on the amount of free time.

I think that life starts outside the comfort zone, going beyond it makes us gain valuable experience, get to know interesting people and develop new skills. It is also important to me: honesty, sincerity and responsibility these values are crucial when working in a group, because they have an impact on the atmosphere and building trust, which undoubtedly translates into results. I believe that nothing is impossible, that's why I am eager to undertake new challenges.

I work professionally with a training-consulting company creating graphic materials (animations, ebooks, infographics, layouts of training materials) and formatting, editing texts training. Mainly I work in programs: Piktochart, Flipsnack, Vyond and Microsoft Office and partly in programs from the Adobe family.

If You want to work with me, hire me - contact me - I'm eager to meet and talk about the details.
